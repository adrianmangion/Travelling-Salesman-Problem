Distance Standard: Euclidean Distance

The first integer in the line is the city number.
The second and third integers are the X and Y coordinates of the city.

Example:

1 1 1    (City 1, x = 1,  y = 1)
2 5 5    (City 2, x = 5,  y = 5)
3 10 3   (City 3, x = 10, y = 3)
4 2 7    (City 4, x = 2,  y = 7)
